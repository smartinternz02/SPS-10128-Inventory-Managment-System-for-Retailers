About us

Online inventory management software is accessed through the internet, rather than through a program installed on your physical computers and servers. This greatly reduces your company’s hardware requirements. What’s more, the subscription-based pricing plans these systems typically offer tend to be less expensive than the traditional, desktop-based systems. This makes online inventory management software a cost-effective option for small businesses.

Inventory managers are uniquely useful for sales departments because they ensure you can easily manage and track your inventory. These platforms include features for monitoring amounts of each product your inventory, including how much is being produced daily, how much is being stored and how much is being sold. With the automation of this information, you can more swiftly monitor inventory data, such as how well certainitems are selling, and more accurately predict future inventory needs.

Real-time inventory management

Allows you to see your stock levels, incoming and outgoing orders, shipping information and a whole host of other measures in real time. This enables you to improve tracking and make informed decisions using up-to-date knowledge of your inventory.

Item variations

This categorization capability is an essential function of most online inventory management systems. It allows you to store key product variations and differentiations in order to quickly and conveniently organize your inventory based on specific details, such as product color.

Scanning

To more easily identify and input products in the system, many solutions use barcode or radio frequency identification (RFID) tags. Scanning these tags with barcode scanners or other devices provides information on the product, as well as actions that can be taken to manage it.

Reporting and analytics

Provides access to user-friendly, data-driven dashboards articulating inventory levels, sales reports and other information you need. With this information in hand, you can make smarter decisions and optimize key components of your business, including pricing, shipping, purchasing and reordering.

Multichannel inventory synchronization

You may sell your products through multiple channels, including online and retail stores. The solution should be capable of providing the exact inventory status by checking all the channels where sales are occurring. Also, inventory should be synchronized with the order management module that notifies customers immediately if any product is out of stock.

Automatic updates

Most tools offer real-time inventory status updates, as inventory management is the most critical task in running a business. It allows managers to check that inventory is stocked and fulfilled from warehouses for every order. Also, in case of low inventory or out-of-stock items, an automatic update is sent to warehouse managers that informs them to take action and order new inventory.
